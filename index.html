Frequently asked
questions

Straight answers about desert HVAC — no upsell, no jargon.

How fast can you get to me in an emergency?

Our average emergency dispatch across the valley is 22 minutes, with techs stationed in Las Vegas, Henderson and Summerlin. The 24/7 line — (702) 555-0148 — is answered by a human, not a voicemail, every hour of the year.

What does a new AC system cost in Las Vegas?

Most full replacements land between $7,500 and $14,500 depending on tonnage, SEER2 rating and ductwork condition. We quote flat-rate after a free in-home load calculation, and we file NV Energy rebates (up to $1,300) on your behalf.

How often should HVAC be serviced in the desert?

Twice a year — a cooling tune-up in spring and a heating check in fall. Mojave dust clogs coils and filters roughly twice as fast as the national average, which is why Vega Shield includes both visits and a filter program.

Do you service all brands?

Yes. Our techs are factory-trained on Trane, Carrier, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, Daikin and Mitsubishi, and our trucks stock universal and OEM parts covering roughly 90% of the systems installed in Clark County.

Is financing available?

Approved customers can spread installations over 12–120 months, including 0% APR promotional terms on select high-efficiency systems. Applying takes about five minutes and doesn't affect your credit score.

Are your technicians certified and insured?

Every tech is NATE- and EPA 608-certified, background-checked and covered by our $2M liability policy. Vega Climate is a licensed Nevada contractor — #C21-0087421 — bonded and insured for residential and commercial work.
