Good to know

AC repair questions

Do you charge extra for nights and weekends?

Our emergency line runs 24/7 with no overtime surcharge for Vega Shield members. Non-members pay a flat after-hours dispatch fee that is credited back if you approve the repair.

My AC runs but blows warm air. What is it?

In the valley it is usually one of three things: a failed capacitor, low refrigerant from a leak, or a dust-choked condenser coil. All three are same-visit fixes for a stocked truck — and all three show up instantly on our gauges.

Repair or replace — how do you decide?

We use the 5,000 rule: multiply the repair cost by the system age; over $5,000, replacement usually wins. We show you the math and quote both paths — the choice is always yours.
