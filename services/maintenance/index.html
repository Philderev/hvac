Maintenance questions

Is maintenance really worth it in Vegas?

DOE data puts efficiency losses from a neglected system at 5% per year — and in our dust that curve is steeper. Members also avoid the mid-July queue, when emergency slots go first to Shield households.

What is included in the 21-point tune-up?

Coil wash, refrigerant verification, electrical inspection under load, blower and airflow balance, thermostat calibration, drain treatment, duct static pressure and a written health score with photos — among other checks.

Can I cancel my plan anytime?

Yes. Plans are month-to-month with no cancellation fee. Annual plans include two months free and are refunded pro-rata if you move out of our service area.
