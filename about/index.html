Who we are

Not the biggest crew.
The most precise one.

Vega Climate takes its name from the brightest star in the northern sky and from the city we call home. We are a team of 38 NATE-certified technicians, engineers and dispatchers who believe a comfort system should be measured, not eyeballed.

Every truck carries thermal imaging cameras, digital manifolds and calibrated airflow instruments. Every job from a $180 capacitor swap to a full VRF retrofit ends with a data-backed report you can read in two minutes.

Our pledge is simple: flat-rate pricing agreed before work starts, techs who treat your home like a cleanroom, and a 100% satisfaction guarantee we have honored since day one.

What we stand for

Four principles.
Every job. No exceptions.

Show up fast, show up ready

22-minute average emergency dispatch, and trucks stocked so deep that 90% of repairs finish on the first visit. Your July should never depend on a parts warehouse's opening hours.

Measure everything

Superheat, subcool, static pressure, delta-T numbers before opinions. If we can't prove a fix with readings, we don't call it fixed.

Price it before we touch it

Flat-rate menus approved on a tablet before a panel comes off. No hourly meters running, no surprise line items, no "while we were in there."

Leave it better than we found it

Shoe covers, drop cloths, magnetic hardware trays and old equipment recycled to EPA standards. Respect for your home is part of the spec sheet.
