Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Vega Climate Systems LLC ("Vega Climate," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or request service, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly — such as your name, phone number, email address, service address and details about your HVAC system — when you submit a quote request, book a service, or contact us. We also automatically collect limited technical data (such as browser type, device, and pages visited) through standard web analytics to help us improve the site.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to quote requests and schedule service appointments.
  • Communicate with you about appointments, estimates and follow-up care.
  • Improve our website, services and customer experience.
  • Comply with legal, tax and licensing obligations.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate — such as scheduling, payment processing and analytics vendors — and only as needed to deliver our services, or when required by law.

Cookies and analytics

Our site uses essential cookies and privacy-respecting analytics to understand traffic and improve performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; some features may not function as intended if you do.

Data security and retention

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information, and we retain it only as long as needed to provide services and meet our legal obligations.

Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may opt out of marketing messages at any time by contacting us. We will honor verified requests as required by applicable law.

Children's privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
